About the Dodge Viper ACR

The Dodge Viper ACR (American Club Racer) is pure, undiluted American muscle on a track. Its massive 8.4-litre V10 engine produces 645 horsepower with no turbochargers, no superchargers, no hybrid system — just raw displacement. The ACR holds more than 13 production car lap records at various racetracks worldwide, including the legendary Laguna Seca and Virginia International Raceway. It's considered one of the greatest track-day cars ever built.

Fun Fact

The Viper ACR was so loud that Dodge had to fit a special exhaust system just to be allowed into certain racetracks. The car's aerodynamic package — including its massive rear wing — generates more downforce than many actual race cars.
