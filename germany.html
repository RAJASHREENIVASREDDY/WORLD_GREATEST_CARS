About the Porsche 911 GT3

The Porsche 911 GT3 is the track-focused version of the iconic 911. It uses a naturally aspirated 4.0-litre flat-six engine — one of the last of its kind — that screams to 9,000 RPM. Unlike most modern sports cars, the GT3 has no turbocharger, making every horsepower feel pure and immediate. The rear-engine layout gives it unique handling characteristics that drivers either love deeply or find challenging.

Fun Fact

The Porsche 911 has been in continuous production since 1963 — over 60 years. It's one of the longest-running car models in history. Every new 911 is instantly recognizable yet completely modern.
