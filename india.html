About the Tata Altroz Racer

The Tata Altroz Racer is India's most exciting hot hatchback, launched in 2023. It uses a 1.2-litre turbocharged petrol engine producing 120 horsepower — significant for a vehicle in its class and price point. Tata Motors has transformed its image dramatically in recent years, winning 5-star Euro NCAP safety ratings and launching electric vehicles. The Altroz Racer represents India's growing confidence in building driver-focused performance cars for the domestic market.

Fun Fact

Tata Motors is India's largest automobile manufacturer and also owns Jaguar and Land Rover — two of Britain's most prestigious car brands! So when you buy a Jaguar in England, it's owned by an Indian company.
