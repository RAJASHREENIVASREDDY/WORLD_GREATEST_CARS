About the Ferrari SF90 Stradale

The Ferrari SF90 Stradale is Ferrari's most powerful road car ever built. It combines a twin-turbocharged V8 engine with three electric motors in a plug-in hybrid system, producing a staggering 986 horsepower. The name SF90 honours Ferrari's 90th anniversary in Formula 1. Every Ferrari is hand-assembled at the Maranello factory in Italy — a process that takes weeks per car.

Fun Fact

Ferrari intentionally limits production to around 14,000 cars per year to maintain exclusivity. If you order a Ferrari today, you may wait 2-3 years for delivery — and Ferrari can choose to refuse your order if they don't think you fit the brand.
