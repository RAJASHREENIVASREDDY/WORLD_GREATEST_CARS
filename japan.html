About the Nissan GT-R R35

The Nissan GT-R R35 is Japan's answer to European supercars — available at a fraction of their price. Its 3.8-litre twin-turbocharged V6 sends power to all four wheels through Nissan's ATTESA E-TS AWD system, giving it extraordinary traction and stability at high speeds. The GT-R gained the nickname "Godzilla" from the Australian motoring press for its ability to destroy much more expensive competitors on any track.

Fun Fact

Every GT-R engine is hand-built by a single master craftsman called a Takumi. Each Takumi's name plate is attached to the engine. It takes approximately one hour to assemble each engine in a dust-free, climate-controlled room.
