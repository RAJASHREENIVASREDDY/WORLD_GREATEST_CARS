About the McLaren P1

The McLaren P1 is one of the most revered hypercars ever built. It pairs a 3.8-litre twin-turbocharged V8 with an electric motor to produce 916 horsepower combined. Only 375 units were ever built, making it extremely rare. McLaren developed it using direct Formula 1 technology — it features active aerodynamics, carbon fibre body panels, and F1-derived suspension. It can lap the Nürburgring faster than almost any road car.

Fun Fact

All 375 P1 units sold out before the car was even officially unveiled to the public. McLaren had a waitlist from existing customers who trusted the brand completely — a remarkable achievement in automotive history.
